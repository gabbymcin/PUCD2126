REJJIE SNOW

Alex Anyaegbunam is Rejjie Snow. Born and raised in Dublin, a place, according to the rapper, of “freedom, loyalty and pain”, a football scholarship took him to Atlanta where he soon discovered his love of music and headed straight to L.A. Now back on our side of the Atlantic and the talented young rapper has been drip-feeding us tracks for the past couple of years while supporting the likes of MF Doom, FlyLo, Kendrick Lamar, and Action Bronson.
