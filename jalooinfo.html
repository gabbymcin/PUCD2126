JALOO

Jaloo is a Brazilian singer-composer originally from Castanhal, Pará. The 28 years old artist presents his type of music as Sci-fi brega, a mixture between tecnobrega and 8-bit electro, and latin rythms, taking as main reference the suburbs of Belém, Brazil, and the world. The essence of his work is a mixture: a game with music from the African ghettos, the shantytowns from Rio de Janeiro, the European clubs, experimental artists and mainly the music made to dance in the lowlands of Pará's capital: the Tecnobrega, a post-modern hybrid that by itself is already a blend of brega music and electro music, where technologic elements and DIY are the basic rules. A music style born in the Belém suburbs that nowadays is the most vanguardist genre in the Brazilian music. Provided with an approach and own design when it comes to making audiovisual that highlights its uniqueness.
