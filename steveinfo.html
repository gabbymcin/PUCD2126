STEVE LACY

Steve Lacy, 18-year-old guitarist and vocalist for The Internet, has shared a debut "song series," Steve Lacy's Demo. With the release, Lacy becomes the third member of the band to drop a solo project this year, following Syd's Fin and Matt Martian's Drum Chord Theory. The project is headlined by a pretty serious pair of jams, "Some" and "Dark Red." Listen here.
