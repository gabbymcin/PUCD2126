TIFFANY GOUCHE

Hailing from a family of renowned musicians, Inglewood, CA native Tiffany Gouché has demanded her way to the forefront of the independent R&B/Soul scene for the past 3 years. With her 2014 Fantasy EP, and her 2015 Pillow Talk EP, the singer/songwriter/producer unapologetically brings authenticity to her music and has a refined sound that has garnered her many praises and accolades by music’s elite. Known for her jazzy, futuristic-soul sound with a hip-hop influence, Tiffany has been a key innovator in the resurgence of traditional R&B. Named by Okayplayer as a Top Artists to Watch in 2017, the knob-twiddling multi-instrumentalist has been blazing the West Coast indie scene collaborating and touring with the UK’s charismatic, emcee/musician Little Simz and sharing stages with free-spirited genre-benders Anderson Paak, Solonge, KING, JMSN, and Soulection. Most recently opening for Ms. Lauryn Hill at the Los Angeles stop of her tour, Tiffany showed us that there is not a crowd that she cannot compel.
