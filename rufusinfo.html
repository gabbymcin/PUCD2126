RUFUS DU SOL

This Sydney alternative-dance band is already pretty huge in Australia, with their first album, Atlas, reaching number one on Australia's ARIA music charts in 2014. The festival friendly group are set to drop their next album, Bloom, on Jan. 22, and is also playing Coachella in 2016. If you're wondering about the double name, it's because they're actually called RÜFÜS, but go by RÜFÜS DU SOL in North America where there is already a band with their original name.
