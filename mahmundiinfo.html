MAHMUNDI

Marcela Vale (Rio de Janeiro), known by the stage name Mahmundi, is a Brazilian singer and songwriter. In a style of her own, she flirts with electronic music, indie, lo-fi and Brazilian reflexive poetry, as well as influences from the music of the eighties. It was compared to Marina Lima and Rita Lee. Before the project Mahmundi, Marcela was a vocalista, guitarist and composer in the Old Irish band. She left the band by her own will in October 2010. Also it worked like audio technique in Circo Voador, like way to exercise her musical talent, but left the cultural space to dedicate to her EP.
