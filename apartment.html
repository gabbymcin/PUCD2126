The perfect apartment for you!

Manhattan Apartment Review is an mobile app that aids users in finding Apartment reviews in three of Manhattan's most popular areas (Midtown, Chelsea and SoHo). Manhattan Apartment Review was founded by McIntosh and Associates in 2009 and strives to obtain as many apartment reviews throughought the Manhattan area as possible. If you are a resident in Manhattan, please contribute by submitting a review to the apartment you currently or previously resided in.
