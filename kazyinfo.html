KAZY LAMBIST

Hailing from France’s deep South - Montpelier - Kazy is a twenty something singer and songwriter making laid back, sunny disco with a nice electro edge. The groove of the beat and the whispered licks of “do you wanna go down?’ on his latest single “Doing Yoga” establish a summer romance vibe that runs through Lambist’s tracks. His own singing style has that uncomplicated, nonchalant delivery that acts such as the Whitest Boy Alive and Air do so well.
